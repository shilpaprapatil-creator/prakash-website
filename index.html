About & Leadership

Prof. Dr. Prakash Sheelvanthmath currently serves as Pro Vice Chancellor, Dayananda Sagar University, Bengaluru, where he leads strategic initiatives across academic excellence, AI-powered research infrastructure, and industry-aligned education.

A senior academic leader with experience across large education ecosystems, he has held leadership roles at East Point Group of Institutions, Chandigarh University, Mohan Babu University, and NIIT Ltd.

His work focuses on transforming universities from degree-centric institutions into research-intensive, innovation-driven, industry-integrated ecosystems.

“Universities must evolve from teaching centers into discovery engines — where talent, technology, and industry intersect.”
